Current Research

I am interested in exploring commonness and rarity using  macroecological approachs to explore how life history and phylogeny are related to ecological and anthropogenic commonness and rarity.

Check out my GitHub repository for more information or to see how progress is going.

I have also been working with collaborators on a macroecological life-history trait database for birds, mammals, and reptiles, which we hope to make publicly available soon.


I'm also working on developing a remote avatar that I can use to attend conferences and workshops.
